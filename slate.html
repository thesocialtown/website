SLATE

Slate (Social Learning Academy for Transformation and Empowerment) is an online learning platform that empowers you to ‘learn, unlearn and relearn’ – the most successful mantra in this fast paced and uncertain era.

It is a platform where we provide an opportunity for collaborative, interactive and practical gain of knowledge and skills. Come, learn, unlearn, and relearn with us!.


  • Go to Slate



We offer quality online programs relevant to the social sector, including live online classroom sessions by highly qualified professionals, live master classes and mentorship sessions from industry leaders, well known social workers, and change agents.

SLATE also provides social mentorship programs and opportunities for internships or volunteering with leading NGOs in the social sector through The Social Town platform.

SLATE also gives you access to a wide variety of quality content including reading material, audio books and articles, and relevant video content.


Through SLATE we focus on providing an opportunity for collaborative, interactive and practical gain of knowledge and skills. We have curated fast paced, industry focused learning programs for the social changemakers to create measurable impact in their work.

SLATE encourages self learning, we recognise you are self-motivated and as leaders you want to take advantage of collective knowledge. We hope this will create a structure for individuals, organizations and philanthropies to align towards a common goal.

The courses provided will focus on technical, managerial, marketing, finance and other skills that relate to social work, social development, disaster management, community building, grant writing etc. Each of the listed courses will be designed, compiled and taught by experts in the respective fields.

Upon course completion you will be awarded a certification by SLATE and the organisations that have partnered with The Social Town. All of the institutions or courses will be thoroughly vetted for quality and content.

About The Social Town

The Social Town is an online networking platform for community builders and social changemakers. Here they can connect, learn about each other, assist, support, exchange resources and offer services.
