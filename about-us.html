The Social Engineering Repository

A collaborative space for socially responsible NGOs, NPOs, Corporates, Government Organisations and Individuals to congregate for better Social Engineering.


A platform that allows people to volunteer their time, skill, expertise and talent ; NGOs and NPOs to find volunteers and funding ; Companies to identify organizations, volunteers and areas to spend their CSR funds ; Government Organizations to direct and utilize the resources available here ; Social Startups to showcase their products and services. In short, a repository of Social Engineering Resources.

What are our hopes and dreams?

Build a committed and collaborative non profit - work culture in India.


Connect a large group of dedicated organisations, individuals, and funders; and actively engage with them to change the course of our communities for the better.


Create a positive change in community building through greater accountability.

What problems are we solving for the greater good?

Bridging the gap between motivated change makers, and organisations who truly believe in ‘giving back’ to the community.


Creating an accountability-culture through effective best practice guidelines in the NGO sector and among government stakeholders.


Establishing and sustaining a culture of excellence among various organisations and individuals who work through The Social Town.


Who and what are we inspiring to change?

The Social Town aims to be an inspirational space. We focus on inspiring our volunteers, funders, and organisations to continue and/or accelerate their zeal towards making change.


Through our work of connecting various disparate parties, we will create a community of like-minded individuals who will remain motivated towards their social commitment.


Through the work facilitated by The Social Town, we will strive towards deriving more benefits out of the existing schemes set up by governmental organisations and community development initiatives driven by corporate institutions

What advantage do we provide?

The Social Town is the only technology platform that brings together all social stakeholders, tools and resources under one roof which


Increases community engagement
Enhances tranparency and accountability
Expands impact
Provides easier access to all resources during disasters and pandemics
Eliminates boundaries and encourages collaboration remotely
