A Networking Platform for Community Builders

Technology as an equalizer and enabler of change

The Social Town (Social Town Foundation) is a non profit foundation registered as a Section 8 company under the Ministry of Corporate Affairs. A virtual platform for community builders and social change makers where they can Iearn about each other, assist , support and exchange resources.

Our intention is to bring all tools and resources for social development under one roof, so that they can collaborate, innovate and impact. The Social Town is currently incubated at NSRCEL Indian Institute of Management Banglore..

Meet Our Founder

Neenu Rathin

Founder & CEO

Entrepreneur | Consultant | Social Catalyst



A compelling social catalyst , Neenu Rathin has been working in the community building and nonprofit sector since 2013. She is an Engineering Graduate in Electronics and Communications Engineering with specialization in Embedded Systems Designing. She also holds a MBA in Marketing and has successfully completed the Executive Management Development Programme in Project Management from IIM Kozhikode. With strong project management and business development skills, Neenu brings with her an analytical bent of mind and a spirit to create impactful programs for social change. The Social Town was born out of that desire to combine various impactful programs, social innovation, and usher in a new way of living and doing.
